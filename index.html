Our Story

A Culinary Journey Born from Passion

Urban Fork was founded in 2009 by acclaimed chef Arun Perera, who trained under legendary Parisian chefs before bringing his refined craft to a new home. His vision was simple: to create a restaurant where every guest feels like a cherished friend.

Over fifteen years, we have grown from a intimate twenty-seat bistro into an award-winning destination restaurant, whilst remaining true to our founding philosophy — that great food demands great ingredients, great technique, and above all, great heart.

Today, Urban Fork holds two prestigious dining awards and continues to push the boundaries of French-Mediterranean cuisine with seasonal menus that celebrate local producers and global inspiration.
